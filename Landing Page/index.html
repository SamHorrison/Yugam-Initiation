About Yugam YUGAM ’22 - The cosmic Techno-Cultural gala of the students, by the students, and for the students. The reason for Yugam reaching a pinnacle is the persevering efforts marshalled by the students in every aspect from organising to managing the smooth conduct of events. This marks the 10th edition of the fest. Yugam is overwhelmed by synergistic workshops, endless entertainment and bustling stalls. It provides a platform for sports enthusiasts, technocrats, and artists to portray their talents. Nothing is more captivating and sensational than a revival, and that's YUGAM ’22.

Moto Yugam enhances the management and organizing skills of every individual working for it. It also brings out the hidden talents of the student community. Students get to know the power of working as a team and progress individually through manifold attributes needed for evolving as a true leader like team building, management, patience, communication, creativity, decision making, delegation, innovation and a lot more. Apart from these qualities, Yugam transforms every student by instilling self confidence, courage, passion, integrity, humility, positivity and many more. The list never halts!

Through the Years

Yugam - A Colossal Congregation of Youth Yugam brings together people for days of celebration and superlative experience. Yugam makes Kumaraguru a locus of scholastic learning and innovation for diverse individuals from all over South India. The main driving force being the dedication, hard work, ardour and zeal of the students, Yugam is a fest crafted over a period of several months to bring out the best of Kumaraguru. Through the years, Yugam has made a huge impact and an indelible imprint in the hearts and lives of thousands of people. For the first time ever since its genesis, Yugam went online in 2021. Even though we were in the midst of a great pandemic, we found a way to give back to society what we take from it. The ‘Make a Child Smile’ campaign illuminated the lives of over 200+ children by providing them with basic amenities. ‘Eegai with Ikigai’ helped many children by enabling them to get one step closer to what they want to become. ‘Mission Aagaaram’ – a campaign to support small businesses around Coimbatore. We had several workshops and events which provided a platform for students from different parts of India. YUGAM ’22 is here again, a brand-new reawakening, a renascence, to carry forth the legacy of Kumaraguru.
